FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions about Sky’s Coffee
  1. What are your operating hours?
    1. We are open from Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on weekends from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
  2. Do you offer WiFi for customers?
    1. Yes, we provide free WiFi for all our customers. Just ask our staff for the login details.
  3. Can I order ahead for pickup?
    1. Absolutely! You can place your order through our website or by calling us directly. We'll have it ready for you when you arrive.
  4. Do you have options for customers with dietary restrictions?
    1. Yes, we offer a variety of options for customers with dietary restrictions, including gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan choices. Please ask our staff for assistance in finding the right option for you.
  5. Do you offer catering services for events?
    1. Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your catering needs.
  6. Can I bring my own reusable cup for my coffee?
    1. Absolutely! We encourage our customers to bring their own reusable cups to help reduce waste. Plus, you'll get a discount on your drink when you do so.
  7. Are you hiring?
    1. We're always on the lookout for passionate individuals to join our team. Check our website or visit us in-store for any current job openings.
  8. Do you have a loyalty program?
    1. Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards our frequent customers with special discounts and offers. Ask our staff how to sign up during your next visit.
  9. Can I reserve a table for a large group?
    1. Yes, we accept reservations for large groups. Please contact us in advance to make a reservation and discuss your requirements.
  10. Do you offer gift cards?
    1. Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They make the perfect gift for any coffee lover!

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us at skyscoffeeid@gmail.com or speak to one of our friendly staff members in-store.
